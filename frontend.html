Premier State Owned Development Bank in Sri Lanka

The 100% State owned bank was set up with the objective of improving the living standards of the rural masses by providing them accessible and affordable credit facilities that in turn would contribute to strengthen the rural economy.
Established under the Pradeshiya Sanwardana Bank Act No.41 of 2008
